The bots

ServerMate

A partner bot that allows you to broadcast your bump advertisement message to its servers every 20 mins! Also with Global Chat

DSC

An easy to use advertising bot that broadcast your bump advertisement to other advertising servers

Bump Central

Bump Central is a bot meant to help your server growing by using it's highly customizable bump commands.

Kanna Bump

A bot that allows you to advertise your discord server to over 23,000+ users every 24 hours. Plus an easy to use AutoBump feature

Automator

Automator is a bot that automatically partners your server with other servers every hour. Simply set up an ad and you're good to go!

What feature do all bump bots need?

To be fair, hours or days of cooldown seems odd...

Since these bots post to the same listing servers, it normally takes about 5 mins for another bump to arrive, hiding someones previous bump and then they have to wait for hours to be able to be on the list for a short amount of time. Result: Hardly any members gained over time and an upset owner...
